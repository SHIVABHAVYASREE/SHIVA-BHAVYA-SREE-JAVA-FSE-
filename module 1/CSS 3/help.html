Help Center

Find answers to common questions about using the Community Event Portal

Finding Events

Browse through our events section to find upcoming community activities. You can use the "Find Nearby Events" feature to locate events close to your current location.

How do I find events near me?

Click on the "Find Nearby Events" button on the homepage. The system will request permission to access your location. Once granted, it will display events in your vicinity.

Can I search for specific types of events?

Yes, you can filter events by type using the dropdown menu in the Events section.

Registration Process

To register for an event:

  1. Navigate to the Events section
  2. Fill out the registration form with your details
  3. Select the event type you're interested in
  4. Submit the form
  5. You'll receive a confirmation message on the page

Is my personal information secure?

Yes, all personal information is encrypted and stored securely. We never share your information with third parties without your consent.

User Preferences

The portal remembers your preferred event type for future visits. If you want to clear your saved preferences, use the "Clear Preferences" button on the registration form.

What information is saved in my browser?

We store your preferred event type in your browser's local storage. This helps personalize your experience when you return to the portal.

How do I clear my saved preferences?

Click the "Clear Preferences" button on the registration form to remove all saved data.

Contact Support

If you need additional help, please use the Contact form to send us a message. We'll respond to your inquiry as soon as possible.

What is the typical response time?

We aim to respond to all inquiries within 24-48 hours during business days.

Troubleshooting

Common issues and their solutions:

  • Images not loading: Check your internet connection or try refreshing the page.
  • Location services not working: Ensure you've granted location permissions in your browser.
  • Form submission errors: Make sure all required fields are completed correctly.
